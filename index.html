About Me

I am a postdoc at MPIK, Heidelberg in the Non-thermal Astrophysics group. I research about Gamma-ray Bursts (GRBs) at VHE (E>100 GeV) mainly with the H.E.S.S. telescopes and the HAWC observatory.
I also contribute to the detectability prospects of GRBs for the SWGO Collaboration.
I have put hands on the GRB scheduling system of H.E.S.S. to improve observations of Fermi-GBM alerts. I have work extensively on analysing the data of GRBs observed with both instruments. For HAWC I work also on improvements to the simulation chain and systematics.
Education
