About Me

I am a postdoc at the Gamma-ray group of LAPP/CNRS in Annecy, France. My main interest is the observation of Gamma-ray Bursts (GRBs) at very high energies (>100 GeV). I am currently a member of the LST-CTAO collaboration, profiting from the largest IACT in the world to detect GRBs. Before, I was deeply involved in the operations of the H.E.S.S. telescopes as Science Operations Manager and member of the GRB group. I was also a member of the HAWC and SWGO collaborations, where I developed tools for the analysis of transient events, event reconstruction and detailed/fancy simulations.
Education and experience
  • Postdoc at Gamma-ray Group of LAPP Annecy-le-Vieux, France
  • Postdoc at the Non-thermal astrophysics group at MPIK Heidelberg
  • PhD in Astrophysics (magna cum laude), University of Heidelberg Dissertation title: Search and first detection of very-high-energy photons in gamma-ray bursts: an analysis with HAWC and H.E.S.S. (download) with the supervision Prof. Dr. Jim Hinton. and Dr. Harm Schoorlemmer.
  • Bachelor in Physics, Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México, Degree obtained based on high GPA.
More about me

I am from Mexico City, but spent most of my childhood summers in Chiapas, growing wild and scared of insects, together with my cousins and brothers. I did my bachelor's at the National Autonomous University of Mexico, and even before starting, I got involved and became interested in a novel observatory that was being built close to a volcano in Mexico: HAWC.

I was a summer student at CERN in 2016, studying jet productions in heavy ion collisions. It has been one of the most exciting and fruitful experiences of my life, but it made me conclude that I wanted to continue the path of studying the Universe with astrophysical objects.

In 2017 I moved to Heidelberg to start my PhD with a project centred on studying GRBs, getting mostly only upper limits. This made a big twist in 2018 when we detected GRB 180720B. Then, the fun and chaos started.

I have a beautiful daughter with a similar explosive character as mine. My hobby is having new hobbies all the time. I enjoy taking photos, mostly portraits of my cat and what happens in the streets. I am a music enthusiast, I do DJing, play the guitar and drums, but none at a decent level as I am always busy shifting to another hobby. I am a hydroponics lover and have grown from coriander to zucchinis and Poblano chillies in the cold winters of Geneva. I use what I grow to cook, one of the few persistent hobbies. I have the soul of a do-it-all father, always fixing something at home and risking chopping a finger.
